Akwa-Ibom Menu

Edikang-ikong Soup

Edikangikong soup is arguably the most popular traditional dish from Akwa-Ibom, Nigeria, made with pumpkin leaves, vegetables, meat or fish, and spices.

It is a flavorful and aromatic delight, with slightly bitter taste of pumpkin balanced by seasonings. Its health benefits includes: Boosting immunity, Supporting eye health, Aiding digestion etc.

Afia Efere

Also, know as ‘white soup’, Afia Efere is so named because palm oil is not an ingredient, unlike other traditional soups. It is often cooked with (cat)fish, beef or goat meat.

It is similar to Ofe Nsala, a traditional Igbo soup. It is a nutrient-dense food, offering High vitamin and mineral content from the vegetables, Protein from meat or fish, Antioxidants from the spices.

Editan Soup

Editan soup is cherished for its unique flavor and numerous health benefits. This flavorful soup is made with editan leaves, vegetables, meat or fish, and spices, offering a rich culinary experience.

Preparation involves: Washing and chopping editan leaves, Sautéing onions, tomatoes, and peppers, Adding protein and spices and Simmering until the flavors meld.

Afang soup

Afang soup originates from the Akwa-Ibom region, where afang leaves are abundant. This soup holds cultural significance, often served at special occasions. Its preparation and sharing foster community bonding and tradition.

It's often served with fufu, eba, or rice/plantain for a satisfying meal.

Atama Soup

This aromatic soup is made with palm fruit, vegetables, meat or fish, and spices.

Palm fruit is the main ingredient, rich in vitamins A and C, potassium, and iron. Other ingredients include: Vegetables: onions, tomatoes, peppers, Protein: meat (beef, goat, or chicken) or fish. Spices and seasonings

Ekpang Nkukwo

This pottage dish is made with grated cocoyam wrapped in leaves It's often served with soups like afang, editan, or abak atama for a satisfying meal.

One of its nutritional benefits is high fiber and vitamin content from cocoyam. It health benefits include: Aiding weight management, Reducing inflammation etc.
